# Mithra Build Week video

`mithra-build-week-demo.mp4` is a 160-second, 1920 × 1080 H.264/AAC demo
video with local macOS narration and burned-in captions. It contains no music,
stock media, credentials, or personal data.

The render uses the final Owner and Partner Week in Review captures. Its
privacy comparison uses two distinct, clean synthetic accounts.
